Reserve a spot

When you need convenient medical care for an illness or injury that is not life-threatening, or if you need a flu shot or a school sports physical, MedStar Health Urgent Care is here for you. Please save your spot in line with online check-in.

If you are experiencing a life-threatening medical emergency, call 9-1-1 or go to the nearest emergency room.

Our team of providers diagnose and treat non-life-threatening illnesses and injuries for patients of all ages. We have X-ray services at most locations and laboratory testing available on-site for common illnesses (strep, mono, influenza, urinary infection, COVID-19, and pregnancy). If your provider determines your condition needs further testing such as blood tests or cultures, we will have most results in 3-5 days. Frequently asked questions

  • What services are available?

    Our urgent care centers provide extended-hour access to medical care for acute illnesses and injuries, just check in online or walk in. The services we offer include the following:

    • Illness care for flu, allergies, bronchitis, infections, pneumonia, strep throat, pink eye, sinus infections, cold symptoms, urinary tract infections, yeast infections, stomach ache/vomiting, and more
    • Injury care for broken bones, stitches, sprains (sprained ankle), lacerations/cuts, minor burns, and more
    • Auto accident injury evaluation
    • Sports physicals (Note: If your child has a history of COVID-19, further cardiac referrals may be needed)
    • Physical exams before surgery (Note: Clearance status is determined by your primary care or anesthesia provider)
    • Flu tests and shots (seasonal availability)
    • Vaccinations
    • COVID and other vaccinations
    • Concussion evaluations
    • STD testing
    • X-rays (most locations)
    • Tuberculin skin and blood tests to test for tuberculosis (TB)
    • T-DAP and rabies vaccination

    On-site laboratory is available for strep, mono, influenza, urinary infection, COVID-19, and pregnancy. If your condition needs further testing such as blood tests or cultures, we will have most results in 3-5 days. Some advanced testing for serious illnesses may require a referral to a nearby hospitalâ€™s emergency department.

    • We make every effort to see you as closely to your check-in time as possible. Please understand your arrival time is not an appointment and may be delayed by unforeseeable emergencies. Your place in line may change if another patient has a more serious condition.
    • We can only hold your place in line for 10 minutes past your chosen arrival time.

  • How can I prepare for my visit?

    • Schedule your visit online

    • Bring your photo ID and payment with you to our urgent care center

    • You will pay for your visit including labs, X-rays, etc., before you leave

  • What happens after my visit?

    • If you are referred to a specialist as a result of your visit, you will be notified by secure text message with scheduling information.

    • Any lab results or upcoming appointments can be found in your profile on the Patient Portal. If you have not received lab results after 7-10 days, please call the lab line at 877-704-9292.

    • If you need a letter to return to work or information about ending your quarantine period, please utilize MedStar eVisit.
